E-Commerce Refund Policy

1. Customers can request a refund only within 30 days of the purchase date.

2. The order status must be "Delivered".

3. Digital products are not eligible for refunds.

4. A refund cannot be processed if the order has already been refunded.

5. The customer must provide a valid reason for requesting a refund.

6. If all the above conditions are satisfied, approve the refund.

7. Otherwise, politely deny the refund and explain the reason.
